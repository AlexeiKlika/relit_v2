Michael Dopp, STL

Michael Dopp graduated from St Augustine’s at the University of Toronto with a Master of Divinity (MDiv) and earned a Licentiate in Sacred Theology (STL), with a concentration on the new evangelization, at Sacred Heart Major Seminary in Michigan. Michael has been involved in a variety of ministries dedicated to evangelization and mission projects in Europe, Africa, and North America. He is founder and president of Mission of the Redeemer Ministries, founder of The New Evangelization Summit, and co-director of The Summer Institute in the New Evangelization.

Michael has presented for 18 diocese and over 50 parishes and apostolates, as well as Catholic ministries such as CCO, NET, and Cursillo. He has led presentations for priests, deacons, university students, prayer groups, RCIA, sacramental preparation groups, adult faith formation, and more.

Michael Dopp was a luke-warm Catholic until he encountered the goodness and love of God at World Youth Day in Rome. The fruit of this encounter and his surrender to the mercy of God was the intentional living out of the richness of the faith. Michael soon felt the call to share Christ’s message with others and evangelize.

relit: The Heart of Evangelization is the fruit of Michael's desire to add strategy to the universal call to evangelize. He saw the need for a training program that was accessible to active Catholic, from parishioners to priests, which would form them to be effective evangelists. The key to the new evangelization is forged in the fire of relit.

When he's not busy travelling across North America to share his unique message of training Catholics in how to evangelize, Michael lives in Ottawa, Canada and enjoys reading with a pumpkin spice latte or delighting in the goodness of his wife Linda and daughter Therese.
