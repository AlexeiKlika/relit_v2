"Everything takes time, but we had this initial fire that came right out of relit. Personally, in my own priesthood, in my own ministry, I speak to couples preparing for marriage in a different way.

I sort of face-palmed when I was first going through relit. I'm literally filling out paperwork or talking about ‘Have we decided if we're going to put a star or an angel on top of the Christmas tree? Have we had these serious conversations?'

Rather than 'Do you know Jesus Christ? Are you head over heels in love with Him? Would you give your life for Him?'

So, that's a change I’ve made. I know our baptismal preparation is different. It's less catechetical, more evangelical. The Knights of Columbus are on board, our religious education program.

It's just given us a new lens through which we view everything that we do.”

Fr. Jon Bakkelund, Diocese of Rockford
