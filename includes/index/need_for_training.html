The Need for Training

According to research, only 5-10% of parishioners are engaged with their faith. That means, more than 90% of people at the parish are not!

Right now we're really blessed because there's a tremendous number of new programs and resources that people can use in order to evangelize. But how are we inviting this more than 90% to experience these programs? How can we bring more people to an encounter with Christ?

The percentage is unique to your parish. But what if we trained the 5-10% of faithful, evangelized Catholics how to evangelize the 95%? What impact would we see?

What program teaches the 5% how to reach the 95% within our parishes, using the programs and resources you already have in place? relit: The Heart of Evangelization.
