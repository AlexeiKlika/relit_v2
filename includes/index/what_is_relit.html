relit is the tool to help those who have already had an encounter with Christ share the Faith with others.

  • Provides your parish staff and ministry leaders with practical training and effective tools
  • Simple and engaging 18-session DVD & Participant Guide format (No prior-training necessary!)
  • Flexible schedule: Perfect for a weekend retreat or 6-9 weeks - Whatever works best for your parish!
  • No need to reschedule your current and upcoming programs. relit begins with the leaders of your staff, ministries, and program leaders, giving you the tools to make every ministry more effective.

This Cycle of Evangelization visualizes the stages of faith a person experiences:

  • We witness others’ living out their faith
  • We hear the Kerygma and the Gospels proclaimed
  • We make a decision of faith, leading to conversion in our lives
  • We grow deeper in our faith through faith formation programs, such as bible studies
  • We are called to share the Gospel and become a witness for others

But what prepares and equips us to share the Gospel?
relit fills this missing piece, giving us the tools and preparation to live out our baptismal vocation to mission and evangelization.
