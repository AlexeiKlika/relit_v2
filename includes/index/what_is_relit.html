What is relit?

relit is a tool to help those who have already had an encounter with Christ share the Faith with others.

relit helps you take a step back to look at your parish life and ministries with a new lens, a renewed perspective.

Provides your parish staff and ministry leaders with practical training and effective tools

Flexible schedule: can be done in a weekend retreat

Simple and engaging 18-session DVD & Participant Guide format

No need to reschedule your current and upcoming programs

relit is a simple way to bring people together to learn about an important topic: evangelization.

Clayton Imoo Archdiocese of Vancouver, Canada
